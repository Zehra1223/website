Horizon Impex Inc., headquartered in Atlanta, GA, DBA Mill Direct Blanks, has been at the forefront of the blank apparel industry since 1986. With over three and a half decades of expertise, we specialize in producing and supplying high-quality Regular & Better Blanks to a diverse range of clients, including distributors, decorated apparel suppliers, colleges, retail, and resort stores.

Our journey begins with our partner mills in Karachi, Pakistan, where our team of seasoned designers, merchandisers, production, and logistics experts work hand in hand with you. From product development and sampling to manufacturing and shipping, we ensure that every step is executed with precision, delivering consistent and top-tier products on time.

At Mill Direct Blanks, we draw on 40 years of experience in factories and supply chains to provide high-quality products at low prices. By shipping directly to the nearest port and producing in low-wage countries like Pakistan, we cut costs and pass the savings on to you. Our blank apparel is customizable, offering flexibility in styles, colors, and fabrics to help you stand out. Committed to eco-friendly and ethical sourcing, we ensure our products meet your standards while supporting sustainable practices. Mill Direct Blanks: Quality, affordability, and customization you can trust.
