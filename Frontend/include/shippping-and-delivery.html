Shipping and Delivery

What are your shipping options?

We primarily offer shipping on an FOB (Free on Board) basis from Port Karachi. For our U.S. customers, we also provide the option of DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) directly to your warehouse. Choose the option that best suits your needs!

How can I track my order?

Tracking your order is easy! For registered users, simply log in to your account to view your Purchase Order (PO) history and real-time production status directly from our ERP system.

If you’re not registered or need additional assistance, just reach out to us at info@milldirectblanks.com. We’ll provide you with the latest updates on your order.

At Mill Direct Blanks, we draw on 40 years of experience in factories and supply chains to provide high-quality products at low prices. By shipping directly to the nearest port and producing in low-wage countries like Pakistan, we cut costs and pass the savings on to you. Our blank apparel is customizable, offering flexibility in styles, colors, and fabrics to help you stand out. Committed to eco-friendly and ethical sourcing, we ensure our products meet your standards while supporting sustainable practices. Mill Direct Blanks: Quality, affordability, and customization you can trust.
